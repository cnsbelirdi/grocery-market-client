Frequently Asked Questions

What is grocery market?

We provide fresh and organic products in the fastest and most hygienic way with a wide range of payment methods.

How can I sign up for Market?

You can register by creating an account with your email account.

How is the Grocery Market used?

After registering on our site:

  • Create your profile,
  • Add your address information,
  • Add the products you want to buy to your cart,
  • Choose your address, payment method and complete the shopping process.

How can I pay?

Payments are made in three ways: online credit & debit card, mobile payment and cash on delivery.

You can pay at the door with cash or pos device.

For online payment, your debit or credit card that you have defined in the system only once is sufficient. Nobody, including Getir, can see your credit card information. Your card information is protected by Mastercard's payment infrastructure, Masterpass or BKM (Interbank Card Center).

You can use BKM Express or Paycell for mobile payment.

When you confirm your order, the order amount is automatically deducted from your card.

I wanted to pay but my limit is insufficient or my card was not accepted. What should I do?

Don't worry. You can pay by adding a new debit or credit card.

Shipping Information

Thank you for shopping at our grocery market! We offer delivery and pickup options to make it easy for you to get the items you need.

DELIVERY

We offer delivery to select zip codes within a certain radius of our warehouse. To see if delivery is available to your area, please enter your zip code at checkout.

Delivery fees are calculated based on the distance from our warehouse and the size of your order.

Our delivery team is committed to getting your order to you as quickly as possible. Most orders are delivered within 30 minutes of placing your order.

RETURNS

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact us within 14 days of receiving your order to process a return or exchange. All returned items must be in their original packaging and in the same condition as when they were received.

If you have any questions about our shipping options or need assistance with your order, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer service team. We are here to help!

Thank you for choosing our grocery market!

Payment Information

Thank you for shopping at our grocery market! We offer the following payment options:

  • Credit Card: We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. Please enter your card information at checkout.
  • Debit Card: We accept debit cards with the Visa or Mastercard logo. Please enter your card information at checkout.
  • Mobile Payment: We accept mobile payments through BKM Express and Paycell. Simply select your preferred mobile payment option at checkout.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): You can pay in cash when your order is delivered. Please have the exact amount ready.

If you have any questions or concerns about payment, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Thank you for choosing our grocery market!

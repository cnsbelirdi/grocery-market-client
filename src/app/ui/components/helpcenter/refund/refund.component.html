Refund Policy

At Grocery Market, we are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality products and services. However, if you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer the following refund policy:

  • If the product you received is defective or damaged, you may request a refund within 7 days of receiving the product. To request a refund, please contact us and provide proof of the defect or damage, such as a photo or video.
  • If you are not satisfied with the quality of the product you received, you may request a refund within 14 days of receiving the product. To request a refund, please contact us and provide a reason for your dissatisfaction.
  • If you have made a mistake while placing your order or if you have changed your mind about your purchase, you may request a refund within 24 hours of placing your order. To request a refund, please contact us and provide your order number.

Please note that in all cases, the product must be returned in its original packaging and in the same condition as when it was received. The refund will be issued to the same payment method used to make the purchase.

This refund policy is in accordance with Turkey's consumer protection laws. If you have any questions or concerns about our refund policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
